This is the initiative started by repository owner *[Ashutosh Hathidara](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ashutosh-hathidara-88710b138/)* on LinkedIn. This project contains LinkedIn posts related to learning resources for new Technology, Design, Self Branding, Motivation etc.Repo owner believe that knowledge is the foundation of progress, and by sharing it with others, we can create a better world. As a content creator,he feel a sense of responsibility to share his knowledge and expertise with the audience to help them grow and learn. Whether it's through his articles, social media posts, or other forms of content. So, let's work together to create a better world by sharing knowledge and contributing to society. You can visit website [here](https://ashutosh1919.github.io/truvisory). Website contains Light and Dark mode theme which you can toggle as per your requirement.
